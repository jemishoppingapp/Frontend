# Using JEMI

JEMI is the campus marketplace for Lagos State University. You order
online, pay securely, and collect your items at the campus gate.

## For buyers

1. Create an account with your email, then complete your profile.
2. Browse products, add what you want to the cart, and check out.
3. Pick your gate: LASU Iba Gate or Iyana Iba Gate. Delivery to the gate
   is a flat ₦500 per order.
4. Pay with card, bank transfer, or USSD through Paystack.
5. You get a pickup code. When your order is ready, go to your gate and
   show the code to the seller.
6. Once you have your items in hand, open the order on JEMI and tap
   **Confirm receipt**. That is what releases payment to the seller, so
   only tap it after you have checked your items.
7. If something is wrong, do not confirm. Contact JEMI support first.

Your money is held by JEMI until you confirm. If nobody shows up to hand
over your order within 7 days, it cancels automatically and your money
is refunded to your card by Paystack (refunds take 5 to 10 working days
to appear).

## For sellers

1. Apply at **Sell on JEMI** with your business and bank details. Use a
   fresh email, separate from any buyer account.
2. We review applications within 48 hours.
3. Once approved, sign in to your seller dashboard to list products with
   photos, prices, and stock.
4. When a buyer orders your item, you will see it under **Orders** with
   the buyer's name and the items to prepare.
5. Print the pickup slip if you want a paper reference for the handover.
6. At the gate, check the buyer's pickup code, hand over the items, and
   tap **Mark delivered** in your dashboard.
7. When the buyer confirms receipt, your share (95% of the item price)
   moves to your available balance. JEMI keeps 5% as the platform fee.
8. Payouts go to your bank weekly or monthly, whichever you choose.

Your bank details can only be changed by contacting JEMI admin. That is
deliberate — it protects your payouts if your account is ever
compromised.

## For admins

- Sign in at `/admin/login`.
- The dashboard shows today's orders, revenue, and anything pending.
- Move orders through their stages: confirmed, processing, ready for
  pickup, completed.
- Review seller applications: approve, reject with a reason, suspend,
  or reinstate. Suspending a seller hides all their products instantly.
- Manage users: edit profiles, reset passwords, disable accounts.
- Refund an order from its detail page when needed. Refunds go through
  Paystack and take 5 to 10 working days.
- Orders flagged "awaiting review" had a mixed handover (some sellers
  delivered, some did not). Decide those case by case.
